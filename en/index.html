Get to Know Me!

Hi! I'm Melih, a 3rd year Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science student at the IUT of Annecy. I love transforming ideas into digital solutions. Holder of a DUT in Computer Science (Bac+2), I'm currently finalizing my BUT. I also have Turkish nationality.

As a passionate web developer, I create websites and mobile applications. I also have professional experience in backend web development.

I design interfaces and work on diverse projects, ranging from event management platforms (Helvetics Events) to transportation applications. I'm a proponent of agile methodologies and write clean, maintainable code with appropriate tests.

I'm seeking internship opportunities, work-study contracts, or full-time positions where I could contribute to exciting projects.

Feel free to contact me on LinkedIn or on Instagram. Whether you have an opportunity, want to collaborate, or simply discuss tech, I'm always open to connecting!
