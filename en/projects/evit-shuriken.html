Project Overview

Evit'Shuriken is a 2D game developed with WPF where you play as a ninja. The objective is to avoid shurikens while progressing through a dynamic and immersive environment. This project was designed to offer a fast and intense gaming experience, emphasizing player reactivity and reflexes.

Evit'Shuriken aims to entertain and challenge players while offering a smooth and intuitive user interface. This project is one of my many creations in game development.

Among its features, Evit'Shuriken includes immersive visual and sound effects, enhancing the gaming experience while remaining accessible to a wide audience.

Main Features

Fast and Intense Gameplay

Dynamic gaming experience emphasizing player reactivity and reflexes with progressive challenges.

Immersive Environment

Dynamic 2D environment with immersive visual and sound effects for a rich and engaging gaming experience.

Intuitive User Interface

Smooth and intuitive interface developed with WPF and XAML for optimal navigation and interaction.
