Project Overview

KUBE is a 3D obstacle course game developed with Unity. The player navigates through different levels, avoiding obstacles with increasing challenges at each stage. This project aims to offer an immersive and engaging gaming experience with a modern and dynamic graphical interface.

KUBE is designed to entertain and challenge players, offering a smooth and engaging gaming experience. This project is part of my portfolio of games developed with Unity.

Among its features, KUBE offers advanced 3D graphics and immersive sound effects for a rich gaming experience.
