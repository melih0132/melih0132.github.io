Project Overview

Evit'Shuriken is a 2D game developed in WPF where you play as a ninja. The objective is to avoid shurikens while progressing through a dynamic and immersive environment. This project was designed to provide a fast and intense gaming experience, emphasizing player responsiveness and reflexes.

Evit'Shuriken aims to entertain and challenge players while providing a smooth and intuitive user interface. This project is one of many of my creations in game development.

Among its features, Evit'Shuriken includes immersive visual and sound effects, enhancing the gaming experience while remaining accessible to a wide audience.
