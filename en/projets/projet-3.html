Project Overview

KUBE is a 3D obstacle course game created using Unity. Players must navigate through multiple levels, dodging obstacles that present increasing challenges as they progress. The aim of this project is to offer an immersive and captivating gaming experience with a sleek, modern graphical interface.

Since its launch, KUBE has been praised for its innovative gameplay mechanics and striking visual aesthetics. The game is regularly updated to enhance user experience and introduce new levels and features.

KUBE is designed to both entertain and challenge players, ensuring a fluid and engaging gameplay experience. This project forms part of my portfolio of games developed with Unity.

Among its standout features, KUBE includes high-quality 3D graphics and immersive sound effects for a rich gaming experience.
