Project Overview

DortanApp is a software developed in WPF with a database, intended for recording activities in a town hall. This software allows town hall staff to select or create an activity, then enter the reservation date and other relevant details.

The software was designed to simplify activity management and improve operational efficiency within the town hall. It provides an intuitive user interface and robust data management to facilitate event tracking and planning.

Since its development, DortanApp has received positive feedback for its ease of use and features tailored to administrative needs. The project continues to evolve with regular updates to integrate new features and improvements.

Among its key features, DortanApp offers reservation management with a clear graphical interface and filtering options for simplified navigation.
