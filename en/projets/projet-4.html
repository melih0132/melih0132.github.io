Project Overview

Kube is a 3D obstacle game developed with Unity. Players navigate through various levels, avoiding obstacles with increasing challenges at each stage. This project aims to provide an immersive and engaging gaming experience with a modern, dynamic graphical interface.

Since its release, Kube has been well received for its innovative game mechanics and attractive visual design. The game is continuously improved with regular updates to enhance user experience and add new levels and features.

Kube is designed to entertain and challenge players, providing a smooth and engaging gameplay experience. This project is part of my portfolio of games developed with Unity.

Among its features, Kube offers advanced 3D graphics and immersive sound effects for a rich gaming experience.
