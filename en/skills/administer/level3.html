Level definition

Level 3 (AC33) of the ADMINISTER competency corresponds to advanced administration and security. At this level, I am able to orchestrate complex infrastructures, implement security strategies, and manage high availability.

This level represents my ability to manage complex production infrastructures, ensure system security, and guarantee high service availability.

Critical Learnings for Level 3

• AC33.01 | Participate in the design and implementation of an IS project : Information system design, participation in implementation, IS architecture
• AC33.02 | Measure economic, societal and environmental impacts : Impact analysis, economic assessment, societal and environmental considerations
• AC33.03 | Adapt an information system to needs : System adaptation, IS evolution, response to business needs

Reference situations

HEVA - IS Project Design and Implementation (Work-Study)
Participation in the design and implementation of an information system project in a professional context. Measurement of economic, societal and environmental impacts of the platform, and adaptation of the information system to the evolving needs of the organization.

Professional Experience - Internship and Work-Study Program
