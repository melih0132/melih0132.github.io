Critical Learning (AC16): Simple team collaboration
First year - Semesters 1 and 2

Back to skills
Level definition

Level 1 of the COLLABORATE competency corresponds to simple team collaboration. This competency represents the first level of learning (Critical Learning AC16). At this level, I am able to identify my aptitudes for working in a team, understand the digital ecosystem (understand the digital environment), know statuses, functions and roles in an organization, and acquire basic interpersonal skills (communication, listening, respect).

This level represents my first steps in teamwork, where I learned the basics of collaboration and team communication. These skills were developed during semesters 1 and 2 (first year of BUT Computer Science) through educational resources R1.11 and R1.12, as well as academic projects SAÉ 1.06 and SAÉ 2.06.

Critical Learning (AC) for Level 1

Here are the 4 critical learning items for Level 1:

AC16.01
Identify one's aptitudes for working in a team
Self-assessment (self-evaluation), skill identification (recognition of one's abilities), awareness of strengths and weaknesses (self-knowledge)
AC16.02
Understand the digital ecosystem
Understanding the digital environment (digital world), tools and technologies (tools used), professional context (work environment)
AC16.03
Know statuses, functions and roles
Understanding roles (each person's functions), hierarchical organization (company structure), responsibilities (each person's obligations)
AC16.04
Acquire basic interpersonal skills
Communication (information exchange), listening (attention to others), respect (consideration), basic collaboration (working together)

Traces and contextualized evidence

Here are my traces and evidence for Level 1:

EVIDENCE 1: Report (Word, Zoreto, Oral)

Context: Academic project carried out in the first year (Semester 1) as part of SAÉ 1.06. This was a document research and report writing project requiring identification of my aptitudes for working in a team, understanding the digital ecosystem, and acquisition of basic interpersonal skills.

Trace:

  • Written report (Word) with document research
  • Oral presentation (Zoreto) of the report
  • Teamwork for project completion
  • Documentation available on GitHub

Justification: This project allowed me to discover my aptitudes for working in a team (AC16.01) by carrying out a collaborative project where everyone had a role to play. I also learned to understand the digital ecosystem (AC16.02) by conducting in-depth document research on the digital environment. Finally, I developed my basic interpersonal skills (AC16.04) by communicating effectively with the team and presenting our work clearly. I used resources R1.11 (Document research, Ad hoc investigation report, Projet Voltaire) and R1.12 (Article and oral presentation).

Reflection: This project was my first teamwork experience in an academic context. The main difficulty was understanding how to collaborate effectively with my classmates and present the work clearly. I overcame this difficulty by communicating regularly with the team, distributing tasks, and applying communication techniques taught in resources R1.11 and R1.12. This project showed me the importance of communication and collaboration in teamwork.

EVIDENCE 2: Aqualac (Company hosting a group for a professional seminar in Annecy)

Context: Academic project carried out in the first year (Semester 2) as part of SAÉ 2.06. This was a project to discover the economic and ecological environment requiring team collaboration and professional communication with a real company (Aqualac in Annecy).

Trace:

  • Discovery of the economic and ecological environment
  • Team collaboration to organize a professional seminar
  • Professional communication with Aqualac company
  • Teamwork for project completion

Justification: This project taught me to understand the digital ecosystem (AC16.02) by discovering the economic and ecological environment in which companies operate. I also discovered statuses, functions and roles in an organization (AC16.03) by interacting with a real company and understanding how it was organized. Finally, I developed my basic interpersonal skills (AC16.04) by communicating professionally with the company and collaborating effectively with my team.

Reflection: This project was an enriching experience that allowed me to discover the real professional world. The main difficulty was understanding how to communicate professionally with a company and how to organize a seminar as a team. I overcame this difficulty by preparing exchanges with the company, coordinating work with the team, and applying developed interpersonal skills. This project showed me the importance of professional communication and collaboration in a real context.

Pedagogical reflection and progression
Challenges encountered
The main challenges at this level concerned understanding the digital ecosystem, effective team collaboration, professional communication, and acquisition of interpersonal skills.
Solutions provided
To overcome these difficulties, I studied communication techniques, practiced teamwork, developed my interpersonal skills, and applied methods taught in class.

Progression Level 1 allowed me to acquire the basics of team collaboration. Through projects like SAÉ 1.06 and SAÉ 2.06, I learned to identify my aptitudes for working in a team, understand the digital ecosystem, know statuses, functions and roles, and acquire basic interpersonal skills. These experiences allowed me to develop a solid foundation in collaboration, from communication to understanding the professional environment. This first experience showed me the importance of teamwork and prepared me for the following levels where collaboration becomes more complex.
