COLLABORATE - Level 1

Critical Learning AC16 : Simple team collaboration

Back to skills
Level definition

Level 1 (AC16) of the COLLABORATE competency corresponds to simple team collaboration. At this level, I am able to actively participate in team meetings, use communication tools, and respect deadlines and commitments.

This level represents my first steps in teamwork, where I learned the basics of collaboration and team communication.

Critical Learnings for Level 1

• AC16.01 | Identify one's aptitudes for teamwork : Self-assessment, skill identification, awareness of strengths and weaknesses
• AC16.02 | Understand the digital ecosystem : Understanding the digital environment, tools and technologies, professional context
• AC16.03 | Know statuses, functions and roles : Understanding roles, hierarchical organization, responsibilities
• AC16.04 | Acquire basic interpersonal skills : Communication, listening, respect, basic collaboration

Resources studied in class (S1-S2)

R1.11 : Communication basics
Interpersonal communication, communication techniques, oral and written expression.

R1.12 : Personal and professional project
Personal development, reflection on professional project, interpersonal skills.

SAÉ 1.06 : Discovery of economic and ecological environment
Understanding of professional environment, digital ecosystem, organizations.

Topics covered

• Identification of one's aptitudes for teamwork: self-assessment, skills
• Understanding of the digital ecosystem: technologies, tools, stakeholders
• Knowledge of statuses, functions and roles in an organization: hierarchy, responsibilities
• Acquisition of basic interpersonal skills: communication, listening, respect

Reference knowledge

• Interpersonal communication: active listening, expression, feedback
• Teamwork: collaboration, coordination, sharing
• Digital ecosystem: technologies, tools, communities
• Business organization: structures, roles, responsibilities
• Roles and responsibilities: definition, assignment, monitoring

Content to complete

To be completed with your personal projects and experiences:

Teamwork
Traces and evidence

Communication : Documented exchanges, participation in meetings, and contributions to projects.

Commitments : Respecting deadlines, delivering assigned tasks, and contributing to team objectives.

Pedagogical reflection

Level 1 allowed me to acquire the basics of team collaboration. I learned to actively participate, use communication tools, and respect commitments.
