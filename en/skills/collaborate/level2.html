COLLABORATE - Level 2

Critical Learning AC26 : Complex team collaboration

Back to skills
Level definition

Level 2 (AC26) of the COLLABORATE competency corresponds to complex team collaboration. At this level, I am able to coordinate tasks with colleagues, resolve conflicts constructively, and contribute to technical decisions.

This level represents an evolution in my ability to work in teams, manage complex interactions, and contribute significantly to projects.

Critical Learnings for Level 2

• Coordinate tasks with colleagues : Work distribution, synchronization, coordination
• Resolve conflicts constructively : Mediation, communication, resolution
• Contribute to technical decisions : Participation in decisions, technical expertise, consensus
• Mentor new members : Knowledge sharing, support, training

Reference situations

HEVA - Team Coordination
Coordination with a development team for the HEVA project, task distribution, resolution of technical conflicts, and contribution to architectural decisions.

Group Projects - Technical Leadership
Team coordination for university projects, conflict resolution, contribution to technical decisions, and mentoring of new members.

Company Internship
Collaboration with a team of senior developers, task coordination, participation in technical decisions, and continuous learning.
