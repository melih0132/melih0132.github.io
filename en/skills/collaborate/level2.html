COLLABORATE - Level 2

Critical Learning AC26 : Complex team collaboration

Back to skills
Level definition

Level 2 (AC26) of the COLLABORATE competency corresponds to complex team collaboration. At this level, I am able to coordinate tasks with colleagues, resolve conflicts constructively, and contribute to technical decisions.

This level represents an evolution in my ability to work in teams, manage complex interactions, and contribute significantly to projects.

Critical Learnings for Level 2

• AC26.01 | Situate role and missions in the organization : Understanding the role, positioning in the organization, assigned missions
• AC26.02 | Apply integration approach : Integration process, team adaptation, organizational integration
• AC26.03 | Mobilize appropriate interpersonal skills : Use of relational skills, context adaptation, effective communication
• AC26.04 | Report on one's activity : Reporting, results communication, transparency, activity reporting

Resources studied in class (S3-S4)

R2.13 : Technical communication
Communication in a technical context, documentation, technical presentation.

R2.14 : Personal and professional project
Deepening of professional project, development of interpersonal skills.

SAÉ 2.06 : Teamwork organization
Practical application of teamwork, organization, coordination.

Topics covered

Reference knowledge
