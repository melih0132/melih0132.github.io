Critical Learning (AC26): Complex team collaboration
Second year - Semesters 3 and 4

Back to skills
Level definition

Level 2 of the COLLABORATE competency corresponds to complex team collaboration. This competency represents the second level of learning (Critical Learning AC26). At this level, I am able to situate my role and missions in an organization, apply an integration approach (integrate into a team), mobilize adapted interpersonal skills (communication, negotiation), and report on my activity (report my work).

This level represents an evolution in my ability to work in teams, manage complex interactions, and contribute significantly to projects. These skills were developed during semesters 3 and 4 (second year of BUT Computer Science) through educational resources R2.13, R2.14, R3.13 and R4.06, as well as academic projects SAÉ 2.06, SAÉ 3.01, SAÉ 4.01 and my internship at Helvetics Events.

Critical Learning (AC) for Level 2

Here are the 4 critical learning items for Level 2:

AC26.01
Situate one's role and missions in an organization
Understanding the role (function in the organization), positioning in the organization (place in the structure), assigned missions (entrusted tasks)
AC26.02
Apply an integration approach
Integration process (integrate into a team), team adaptation (adapt to the group), organizational integration (integrate into the company)
AC26.03
Mobilize adapted interpersonal skills
Use of relational skills (communication abilities), context adaptation (adapt to the situation), effective communication (clear information exchange)
AC26.04
Report on one's activity
Reporting (activity report), results communication (present results), transparency (clarity), activity report (work report)

EVIDENCE 1: Aqualac (Organization of a professional seminar)

Context: Academic project carried out in the second year (Semester 2) as part of SAÉ 2.06. This was a project to organize a professional seminar with the company Aqualac in Annecy, requiring team collaboration, professional communication, and event organization.

Trace:

  • Organization of a professional seminar with the company Aqualac
  • Team collaboration for project completion
  • Professional communication with the company
  • Documentation available on GitHub

Justification: This project allowed me to learn to understand my role and missions in an organization (AC26.01) by discovering what was expected of me in organizing this seminar. I also learned to integrate into a team (AC26.02) by adapting my work to collaborate effectively with others. Finally, I developed my interpersonal skills (AC26.03) by communicating professionally with the company Aqualac. I put into practice what I learned in resources R2.13 (Communication with the professional environment) and R2.14 (Professional and personal project).

Reflection: This project was an enriching experience that allowed me to develop my complex collaboration skills. The main difficulty was coordinating teamwork and communicating professionally with the company. I overcame this difficulty by regularly organizing team meetings, distributing tasks, and applying communication techniques taught in resources R2.13 and R2.14. This project showed me the importance of coordination and communication in teamwork.

EVIDENCE 2: Clone Uber (Laravel) - Teamwork, Agile methodology

Context: Academic project carried out in the second year (Semester 3) as part of SAÉ 3.01. This was a complex web application development project with Laravel, requiring teamwork, application of Agile methodology (Scrum), and complex collaboration with task coordination.

Trace:

  • Teamwork with Agile methodology (Scrum)
  • Task coordination with work distribution
  • Complex collaboration with multiple developers
  • Source code available on GitHub (repository uber-laravel)
  • Detailed project sheet (see project sheet)

Justification: This project allowed me to develop my interpersonal skills (AC26.03) by collaborating effectively with the team and coordinating tasks so everyone worked in the same direction. I also learned to report on my activity (AC26.04) by documenting my work and regularly communicating project progress to the team. I used Agile methodology (Scrum) to organize our teamwork in a structured way.

Reflection: This project was my first in-depth experience with Agile methodology in a team. The main difficulty was coordinating tasks between multiple developers and managing complex interactions. I overcame this difficulty by using project management tools (Trello, GitHub), organizing regular meetings, and applying Agile methodology principles. This project showed me the importance of coordination and communication in complex teamwork.

  • Contribution to technical decisions
  • Coordination with multiple technologies (.NET, Vue.js)

Justification: This project allowed me to develop my interpersonal skills (AC26.03) by collaborating effectively with the team on a complex project that used several different technologies. This project showed me the importance of technical communication and coordination in complex projects.
