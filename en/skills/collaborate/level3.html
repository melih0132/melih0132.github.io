Level definition

Level 3 of the COLLABORATE competency corresponds to leadership and advanced collaboration. This competency represents the third level of learning (Critical Learning AC36), the most advanced level. In my Application Development pathway, only the REALIZE, OPTIMIZE and COLLABORATE competencies are evaluated at level 3 in semesters 5 and 6. At this level, I am able to organize and share technological monitoring (surveillance of new technologies), guide change management (accompany transformations), support project management (support project management), and identify economic issues of innovation (understand the economic impact of innovations).

This level represents my ability to take on a leadership role, facilitate large-scale collaboration, and contribute to company culture. These skills are developed during semesters 5 and 6 (third year of BUT Computer Science) through educational resources R5.01, R5.03, R5.06, R5.07, R5.13 and R5.14, as well as academic projects SAÉ 5.01 and my professional work-study experience at Agence Altimax.

Critical Learning (AC) for Level 3

Here are the 4 critical learning items for Level 3:

AC36.01
Organize and share technological and information monitoring
Technological monitoring (surveillance of new technologies), knowledge sharing (dissemination of information), information dissemination (communication of news), monitoring organization (structuring of surveillance)
AC36.02
Identify the economic issues of digital innovation
Understanding of economic issues related to digital innovation, analysis of the economic impact of technologies, identification of opportunities and challenges
AC36.03
Guide the conduct of IT change within an organization
Leadership in change management, support for IT transformations, facilitation of the adoption of new technologies and practices
AC36.04
Support IT project management
Support for project management, contribution to the management and monitoring of IT projects, technical expertise in project support

Traces and contextualized evidence

Here are my traces and evidence for Level 3:

EVIDENCE 1: SmartHome (IoT/AI technological monitoring, team management)

Context: Academic project carried out in the third year (Semester 5) as part of SAÉ 5.01. This is a complex project developing an Android mobile application with Kotlin, integrating IoT (Internet of Things) via MQTT and Raspberry Pi, and using artificial intelligence, requiring organization and sharing of technological monitoring, guidance of change management, and support for project management.

Trace:

  • Organization and sharing of technological monitoring (IoT, AI) with documentation
  • Guidance of change management in the team
  • Support for project management with RACI and conflict management (R5.01)
  • Organized and shared technological monitoring with the team (R5.03)
  • Identification of economic issues via Digital Fresco (R5.13)
  • Application source code (project in progress, confidential)

Justification: This project allowed me to learn to organize and share technological monitoring (AC36.01) by monitoring new IoT and AI technologies and regularly sharing interesting information with the team. I also discovered how to guide change management (AC36.02) by supporting the team in adopting new technologies and helping them understand their usefulness. Finally, I developed my skills to support project management (AC36.03) by using RACI to clarify responsibilities and managing conflicts constructively. I used resources R5.01 (Team management), R5.03 (Technological monitoring) and R5.13 (Digital Fresco).

Reflection: This project is the most complex in terms of leadership and advanced collaboration as it combines several aspects: technological monitoring, change management, and project management. The main difficulty was effectively organizing technological monitoring and guiding the team in adopting new technologies. I overcame this difficulty by structuring technological monitoring, regularly sharing information with the team, and applying team management methods taught in resource R5.01. This project showed me the importance of leadership and technological monitoring in complex projects.
