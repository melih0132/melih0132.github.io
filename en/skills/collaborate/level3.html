Level definition

Level 3 (AC36) of the COLLABORATE competency corresponds to leadership and advanced collaboration. At this level, I am able to lead development teams, facilitate inter-team communication, and promote best practices.

This level represents my ability to take on a leadership role, facilitate collaboration at scale, and contribute to company culture.
