Level definition

Level 2 (AC25) of the CONDUCT competency corresponds to complex project management. At this level, I am able to apply agile methodologies, manage risks and changes, and coordinate a team.

This level represents an evolution in my ability to manage more complex projects, use structured methodologies, and coordinate teams.

Critical Learnings for Level 2

• AC25.01 | Formalize business needs : Needs formalization, functional specifications, requirements documentation
• AC25.02 | Define feasibility criteria : Feasibility analysis, criteria definition, constraint evaluation
• AC25.03 | Implement appropriate monitoring : Monitoring setup, project monitoring, process adaptation
