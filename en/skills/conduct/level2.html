CONDUCT - Level 2

Critical Learning AC25 : Complex project management

Back to skills
Level definition

Level 2 (AC25) of the CONDUCT competency corresponds to complex project management. At this level, I am able to apply agile methodologies, manage risks and changes, and coordinate a team.

This level represents an evolution in my ability to manage more complex projects, use structured methodologies, and coordinate teams.

Critical Learnings for Level 2

• AC25.01 | Formalize business needs : Needs formalization, functional specifications, requirements documentation
• AC25.02 | Define feasibility criteria : Feasibility analysis, criteria definition, constraint evaluation
• AC25.03 | Implement appropriate monitoring : Monitoring setup, project monitoring, process adaptation

Reference situations

HEVA - Complex Project with Agile Methodology
Formalization of business needs for an event management platform, definition of technical and economic feasibility criteria, and implementation of appropriate monitoring with Agile methodology (Scrum, sprints, retrospectives).

Uber Laravel - Complex Project
Formalization of business needs for a transportation application, definition of feasibility criteria, and implementation of appropriate monitoring with priority and deadline management.

Uber Vue.js/.NET - Full-Stack Project
Formalization of needs for a full-stack transportation application, definition of technical feasibility criteria, and implementation of appropriate monitoring with Agile methodology.

DortanApp, Weather App, APIfilms - Medium Projects
Formalization of business needs, definition of feasibility criteria, and implementation of appropriate monitoring for each project with appropriate management tools.
