Critical Learning AC25 : Complex project management

Level definition

Level 2 (AC25) of the CONDUCT competency corresponds to complex project management. At this level, I am able to apply agile methodologies, manage risks and changes, and coordinate a team.

This level represents an evolution in my ability to manage more complex projects, use structured methodologies, and coordinate teams.

Critical Learnings for Level 2

• Apply agile methodologies : Scrum, sprints, retrospectives, daily standups
• Manage risks and changes : Risk identification, change management
• Coordinate a team : Task distribution, team tracking, communication
• Optimize processes : Continuous improvement, workflow optimization

Reference situations

HEVA - Multi-Technology Complex Project
Team coordination for the development of a complete platform with application of Scrum methodologies, management of priorities, deadlines and technical risks.

UberS4 - Large-Scale Project
Management of a complex full-stack project with integration of multiple technologies, application of agile methodologies, and respect of time constraints.

University Group Projects
Team coordination for university projects with application of Scrum, sprint management, and organization of retrospectives.
