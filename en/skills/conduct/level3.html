Critical Learning AC35 : Advanced project management

Level definition

Level 3 (AC35) of the CONDUCT competency corresponds to advanced project management. At this level, I am able to lead strategic projects, establish processes, and ensure project continuity.

This level represents my ability to take charge of strategic projects, establish processes, and guarantee quality and project continuity.

Critical Learnings for Level 3

• AC35.01 | Measure project impacts : Impact assessment, results measurement, value analysis
• AC35.02 | Integrate a project into an IS : Integration into information systems, IS architecture, compatibility
• AC35.03 | Adapt the IS to evolving needs : System evolution, adaptation to changing needs, flexibility
