Level definition

Level 3 (AC34) of the MANAGE competency corresponds to advanced management and big data. At this level, I am able to design data architectures, use NoSQL technologies, and manage data governance.

This level represents my ability to manage complex data architectures, work with different storage technologies, and ensure data governance.

Critical Learnings for Level 3

• AC34.01 | Design and implement decision support systems : Decision support system design, data warehouses, business intelligence
• AC34.02 | Integrate complex data management solutions : Integration of complex solutions, advanced data management, data architectures
• AC34.03 | Take into account regulatory issues : Regulatory compliance, GDPR, data protection, legal issues
