Critical Learning AC31 : Advanced design and development

Level definition

Level 3 (AC31) of the REALIZE competency corresponds to the design and development of advanced applications. At this level, I am able to lead the technical design of a project, optimize performance and security, and ensure system maintenance and evolution.

This level represents my ability to take charge of complex projects end-to-end, make strategic technical choices, and guarantee quality, performance and security of applications in production.

Critical Learnings for Level 3

• AC31.01 | Choose and implement appropriate architectures : Selection of appropriate architectures (microservices, monolithic, distributed, event-driven), implementation and justification of architectural choices, evaluation of technical constraints
• AC31.02 | Evolve an existing application : Refactoring, migration, architecture evolution, technical debt management, reverse engineering, code restructuring
• AC31.03 | Integrate a software solution into production : Production deployment, CI/CD, monitoring, operational maintenance, orchestration, observability
