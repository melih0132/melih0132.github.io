REALIZE - Level 3

Critical Learning AC31 : Advanced design and development

Back to skills
Level definition

Level 3 (AC31) of the REALIZE competency corresponds to the design and development of advanced applications. At this level, I am able to lead the technical design of a project, optimize performance and security, and ensure system maintenance and evolution.

This level represents my ability to take charge of complex projects end-to-end, make strategic technical choices, and guarantee quality, performance and security of applications in production.

Critical Learnings for Level 3

• Lead the technical design of a project : Architectural decision-making, technology choices, technical leadership
• Optimize performance and security : Code optimization, application security, vulnerability management
• Implement CI/CD processes : Complete deployment automation, continuous testing, monitoring
• Ensure system maintenance and evolution : Refactoring, architecture evolution, technical debt management
